Sweet Potato Souffle

Creamy mashed sweet potatoes topped with a buttery brown sugar streusel. This holiday side dish will be met with rave reviews. Good news: yes, this is as delicious as it looks. And no, it's not too soon to start thinking about our holiday menus. I mean, if there was ever a year to eat ALL the comfort food, this is it.The base of this recipe is mashed sweet potatoes. The potatoes are sweetened and combined with eggs, milk, butter, and a touch of vanilla. Mixing the sweet potatoes with a hand mixer or food processor creates a light and fluffy texture that is incredible.

Ingredients:

  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Buttter
  • Eggs
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cream
  • Flour
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt
  • Roasted Pecans

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  2. Grease the baking pan with butter.
  3. Boil sweet potatoes for 15 min, then drain amd mash.
  4. Prepare the souffle, mix butter, cream, eggs, vanilla extract, flour and all remaining ingredients.
  5. Finish the souffle: spoon mashed potatoes into the baking dish, smooth the top, sprinkle with roasted pecans and cinnamon sugar.
  6. Bake for 30 min.
  7. Serve immediately.
